Anastasia "Ana" Bray

"Guardians aren't supposed to investigate their past. That's the rule. But I'm not good with rules. Not when there's this much at stake."

Anastasia Bray, better known as Ana Bray, is a Hunter. She was present at the Battle of Twilight Gap where she wore a cloak with the same pattern as Strength of the Pack.

Anastasia Bray is a member of House of Bray, she was told she was adopted when she was young to avoid telling her of her father's infidelity. She is one of the founders of the exoscience corporation Clovis Bray.Her whereabouts and exploits during the events of the Red War are largely unknown, with the exception of trace mentions by other characters or her redacted diary located in the Daito office in the Tower.

Anastasia Bray played a much larger role back before the events of Destiny, especially during the massive assault by the Fallen, known as the Battle of Twilight Gap, where the houses of Kings, Devils and Winter attacked the Last City all at once. She was in a fireteam with Lord Shaxx, Liu Feng, Nkechi-32, Idil Abdi and Truce during the battle. She engaged in a duel with a Fallen Archon but was thrown off the top of the wall and presumed dead for a long time.
