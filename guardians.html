Notable Guardians

Many guardians have become famous - sometimes infamous - through their incredible feats of power, strength, intellect, and bravery in writing and rewriting the history of the The Last City. These guardians became legends whose stories were and will be spoken for generations. Through their exploits, they have carved their names into humanity's long history of engagement and riumph over its enemies. Those featured here, among many others, no matter the reason, held pivotal roles in shaping the foundation for future guardians in their battle against the Darkness.
