Ikora Rey

Respected among all, Ikora Rey tutors new Warlocks with a lifetime of hard-earned knowledge.

Ikora Rey is the Last City's current Warlock Vanguard, and is the second Warlock Vanguard overall. She took over the position from her former mentor, the legendary Warlock Osiris, upon his banishment from the city--though she had been performing the duties of the anguard long before his banishment was made official.

Prior to taking on her role as Vanguard, Ikora flew solo reconnaissance missions across the inner solar system and was known for being outspoken. At that time, Invective was her weapon of choice. She is also the only person to have ever beaten Lord Shaxx in a Crucible match.[6] Ikora was mentored by the Warlock Vanguard Osiris.

Ikora Rey's personality has many layers to it. To some, she is cold and analytical. To others, she is warm and caring. She has been shown to be more light hearted than Commander Zavala, but not quite as lackadaisical as Cayde-6. She is a very introspective person, showing that in times of great distress (such as the Red War) she will question herself, and how she can be better.While outwardly Ikora seems to be the type to overthink everything, according to her Ghost, she is quite the opposite. Her Ghost often needs to act as a balancing force, so that she does not act impulsively or out of emotional distress.
