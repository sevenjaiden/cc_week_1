Lord Shaxx

A veteran of the Crucible, Lord Shaxx both arms and oversees those who seek to forge themselves within it.

Shaxx is a hero of the Battle of Twilight Gap and led a counterattack against the Fallen that pushed them from The Last City's walls, along with Nkechi-32, Idil Abdi, Truce, Liu Feng and Ana Bray. He and Zavala were both proteges of Lord Saladin, however Shaxx's relationship with Saladin has become strained since the Battle of Twilight Gap. He is still on good terms with Zavala. Shaxx now serves as
the head of the Crucible in the City.
